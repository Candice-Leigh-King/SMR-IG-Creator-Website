Kenya is known as the heartbeat of Africa for a reason. It's abundance of wildlife that graze on the affluent grasslands of the country's many national parks, makes for a safari wonderland to anyone interested to see animals such as the big 5 with the backdrop of Africa's tallest peak. The vastness of Kenya's beauty does not end at the big 5 - it also has some of the best untouched white sand beaches with crystal clear water and thriving coral reefs. Kenya is truly a melting pot of culture with diverse cuisine and wildlife to see every corner you turn.
