Pre-coursework

The Social Media Retreat isn't open for just any type of digital creator! We like our students to meet a standard before arriving at our retreats that's why we are going to have you prepared and ready to go with our pre-course work. We'll build your mindset, grow your brand, and create your engaged community so you are ready to land paid brand deals by the start of this social media bootcamp.

Introduction

Welcome! We'll talk about all the future success that is heading your way and share the overview of your journey through the pre-course leading up to the start of the retreat.

Lesson 1: Personal Branding - Core Identity

In this section we're going to dive deep into the mindset it takes to be a successful brand. We'll teach you how to brand yourself, dos and don'ts, work through your dreams, values and purpose. Then we'll show you the best techniques to make your brand unique and recognizable. You'll have a fun branding activity to complete too.

Lesson 2: IG Growth

Learn how to create a highly optimized and converting profile to boost your discoverability, reach new audiences through IG SEO and maximize follower conversion.

IG Growth Activity

An engagement and growth activity that helps you understand how to create sustainable, organic growth.

Lesson 3: Content Creation (part 1)

In this lesson, we teach you the essentials to keep in mind when creating your content from how to create unique content, where to get inspiration, how to do basic Lightroom color grading edits and minor photoshop edits to take your content to the next level. We'll also go over some video editing apps tips to create reels.

Lesson 4: Content Creation (activity)

A content creation exercise where you'll be able to put into practice what you learned to create unique content.

Lesson 5: Content Creation (part 2)

In this part, we look closely at the photography/videography part of content creation from photography gear, what to keep in mind when doing a photoshoot whether it's inside/outside and different niches (lifestyle, travel, product) as well as some posing tips to get more comfortable in front of the camera.

Bonus lesson

We'll give you some extra tips and tidbits, and show you some of our favorite apps that you can start using right away to make yourself a better creator.

Coursework

The Social Media Bootcamp in Thailand isn't all sun, fun, and island hopping! We actually have a lot of lessons to cover to give you the value you came here for. During this Thailand social media bootcamp, we're going to take you from poor product exchanger to brand deal boss! Your new career begins here and by the end of this journey you'll be making a living doing what you love as a commercial content producer and social media content creator landing paid collaborations from the other side of the world!

Introduction

Get to know your coaches and break the ice with your fellow creators through a series of mindset and motivational activities that will also set you up for success.

Pre-coursework review

Before we can get started on this social media bootcamp we first need to make sure everyone is ready! We'll cover the pre-course work and answer any questions you have so you are confident and ready to dive straight into the new world of creation and online business.

Lesson 1: Personal Branding - Extended Identity

Before you can move forward, you need to have created a well established brand. We'll go over your brand as a person, community and symbol. You will also have time to create your brand manual which will help you A LOT when it comes to your Instagram, content creaton, online strategy and website development.

Lesson 2: Instagram Strategy (part 1) - Content Strategy

Learn the importance of creating a content strategy and having well-defined content pillars. Use the CAGE formula to understand the four types of content you should be creating on Instagram and the importance of copywriting for your content on Instagram.

Lesson 3: Instagram Strategy (part 2) - Going virals on Reels and TikTok

Decode the Reels algorithm and learn what makes a viral-worthy reel. Learn how to plan and batch create your reels to help you save time and deep-dive into what makes a good trend, how to find them, and how to apply them to your own reels. Learn how to explode on TikTok and direct traffic to your Instagram profile.

Lesson 4: Instagram Strategy (part 3) - Networking & engagement

Discover why having a strong network and community is so important and how to differentiate between your tribe and your fans. Learn the journey a stranger takes to become a fan of your brand and keep up with the best practices for engagement.

Lesson 5: Brand and Hotel Collabs

Before we can begin pitching to brands, we need to build our own creator portfolios. In this section we'll cover the 3 things that every creator needs in their portfolio to be professional and successful, and then we'll discuss exactly what brands are looking for in your portfolio so we can cater to those requirements and build them to a highly professional standard.

Lesson 6: Brand and Hotel Collabs Activity

What would a collab course be without hotel collabs activity?! In the Hotel Collabs module. we'll do an activity to prepare you to land hotel collabs so that you can travel anywhere in the world, stay wherever you want for free, make a living doing what you love all while adding value to the tourism industry!

Diversifying your income stream

Understand why it's so crucial for your success to diversify your income stream and learn the most profitable income strategies to start implementing on your personal brand right now. From products to memberships, learn all the business models that are bringing TONS of revenue for creators right now.

Lesson 7: Creating your business

Your website is the front door to your business and in this lesson we'll teach you what a high-converting website should be like. Also opening up a business requires some decisions like the type of business you will need, how to manage taxes, and handle payments.

Lesson 8: Website Development

You'll be trained on how to edit your website and given time to build your website and the kind of information needed to convert viewers into clients.

Lesson 9: Presenting your brand and business

The moment you've been waiting for! You get to present your amazing brand and business to us, so that we can give you positive feedback and suggestions on any improvements.
