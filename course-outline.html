Pre-coursework

The Social Media Retreat isn't open for just any type of digital creator! We like our students to meet a standard before arriving at our retreats that's why we are going to have you prepared and ready to go with our pre-course work. We'll build your mindset, grow your brand, and create your engaged community so you are ready to land paid brand deals by the start of this social media bootcamp.

Introduction

Welcome! We'll talk about all the future success that is heading your way and share the overview of your journey through the pre-course leading up to the start of the retreat.

Personal Branding - Core Identity

In this section we're going to dive deep into the mindset it takes to be a successful brand. We'll teach you how to brand yourself, dos and don'ts, work through your dreams, values and purpose. Then we'll show you the best techniques to make your brand unique and recognizable. You'll have a fun branding activity to complete too.

IG Growth

We will walk you through what kind of person your brand represents so you can attach a unique style, niche, and personality to your brand. Then we'll discover exactly who your ideal follower, audience, and customer is and turn them into a figurative person that will guide your business decisions in all your future content.

IG Growth Activity

Before we can start to collaborate with brands we need to have an engaged community. In this section we'll walk you through the fundamental methods to gain new followers and the best techniques to keep them engaged so that you can have a successful paid collaboration in the future.

Content Creation (part 1)

Finally! Your first taste of what we'll be covering during this retreat! In this section we'll show you exactly how to find the perfect brands to work with so you are ready to start pitching when you arrive in Kenya!

Content Creation (activity)

We'll briefly summarize our pre-course work and make sure you are exactly where you need to be before we start the social media bootcamp so that you can have the best chance at success in your future collaboration opportunities on the retreat.

Content Creation (part 2)

We'll briefly summarize our pre-course work and make sure you are exactly where you need to be before we start the social media bootcamp so that you can have the best chance at success in your future collaboration opportunities on the retreat.

Bonus lesson

We'll give you some extra tips and tidbits, and show you some of our favorite apps that you can start using right away to make yourself a better creator.

Coursework

The Social Media Retreat in Kenya isn't all sun, fun, and safaris! We actually have to do a little work to give you the value you came here for. During this Kenya social media bootcamp we are going to take you from poor product exchanger to brand deal boss! Your new career begins here and by the end of this journey you'll be making a living doing what you love as a commercial content producer and social media content creator landing paid collaborations from the other side of the world!

Introduction

Get to know your coaches and break the ice with your fellow creators through a series of mindset and motivational activities that will also set you up for success.

Pre-coursework review

Before we can get started on this social media bootcamp we first need to make sure everyone is ready! We'll cover the pre-course work and answer any questions you have so you are confident and ready to dive straight into the new world of creation and online business.

Personal Branding - Extended Identity

Before you can move forward, you need to have created a well established brand. We'll go over your brand as a person, community and symbol. You will also have time to create your brand manual which will help you A LOT when it comes to your Instagram, content creaton, online strategy and website development.

Instagram Strategy (part 1)

All about Instagram! Learn how to create content that actually attracts and converts visitors into followers and followers into superfans.

Instagram Strategy (part 2)

Discover the secret recipe for virality on Instagram and TikTok and learn why networking is key for your success as a creator.

Instagram Strategy (part 3)

In order to market branded products to your community and have a hugely successful collaboration we'll learn how to edit product photos to make them beautiful, attractive, and cohesive with your feed, and then use those product photos to build your own portfolio.

Brand and Hotel Collabs

Before we can begin pitching to brands, we need to build our own creator portfolios. In this section we'll cover the 3 things that every creator needs in their portfolio to be professional and successful, and then we'll discuss exactly what brands are looking for in your portfolio so we can cater to those requirements and build them to a highly professional standard.

Brand and Hotel Collabs Activity

What would a collab course be without hotel collabs?! In the Hotel Collabs section we'll talk about the bulletproof method we use to land hotel collabs with a 100% success rate so that you can travel anywhere in the world, stay wherever you want for free, make a living doing what you love all while adding value to the tourism industry!

Diversifying your income stream

In this lesson we'll show you the myriad of ways you can find brands to work with that will be mutually beneficial to you, your audience, and the brand. We'll also cover exactly what brands are looking for when they come to your profile to see if you are the right person for a paid collaboration.

Creating your business

We are going to show you the art of pitching, the very techniques and templates we use to reach out to some of the world's biggest brands, and how sometimes pitches alone aren't enough. We'll show you how to professionally follow up with brand deals and how to create your own contract that cover you during a paid collaboration so that you don't have to find out the hard way when things go wrong. We'll go through dos and don'ts

Website Development

You'll be trained on how to edit your website and given time to build your website and the kind of information needed to convert viewers into clients.

Presenting your brand and business

The moment you've been waiting for! You get to present your amazing brand and business to us, so that we can give you positive feedback and suggestions on any improvements.
